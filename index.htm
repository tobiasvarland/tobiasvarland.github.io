My Gear

When I was a lead photographer, I would tell my clients that I had enough gear and backup gear that if something broke, they wouldn't know it. I see that as my responsibility as a wedding photographer.

I shoot on Nikon D800 cameras. I always bring at least two with me to weddings. When shooting photos, I always shoot on two memory cards at a time in order to create a realtime backup. All of my lenses are genuine Nikon lenses. I have primes (35 f/1.4, 50 & 85 f/1.8), zooms (24-70 & 70-200 f/2.8 (two of each)), and a macro lens (105 f/2.8).

In addition to cameras and lenses, I have pretty much any accessory I can imagine needing at a wedding: flashes, remote triggers, tripods, monopods, spare batteries, video light, etc. In short, I come prepared.
